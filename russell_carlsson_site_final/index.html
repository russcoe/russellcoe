Bio

Russell Carlsson Coe is a composer and sound designer based in Seattle. His music blends ambient textures, experimental techniques, and cinematic storytelling. Drawing from influences like shoegaze, science fiction, synth pop, and ambient, his work explores emotion through texture — pairing soft melodies with distortion, silence with saturation, and analog synths with layered effects.

Russell builds sonic environments that feel both intimate and expansive, like fragments of dreams or memories. His approach centers around atmosphere — crafting pieces that evoke mood, space, and story without needing words.

In addition to his solo compositions, Russell is developing a portfolio of original scores for video games, short films, and live events. He’s always open to new collaborations in sound design, media scoring, and visual storytelling.

If you'd like to work together — or just want to talk sound — feel free to reach out.
